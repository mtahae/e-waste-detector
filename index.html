Recycling

1. RE-USE: Use rechargeable batteries.

  • First if you can REUSE recharge and reuse the battery
  • Buy batteries you can charge again.
  • Use them many times! This is reuse. It helps the planet.

2. RECYCLE: Take old batteries to a special box.

  • When a battery is completely dead, don't put it in the normal bin.
  • Look for the battery recycling box in a big store (supermarket).
  • Put the old battery in the box. This is recycling. It is safe!
